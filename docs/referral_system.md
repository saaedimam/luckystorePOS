# Lucky Store POS - Referral System

**Purpose:** Incentivize current customers to refer new stores through a structured referral program.

**Program Launch Date:** After Pilot Program (Day 91+)

---

## 1. Program Overview

### Objective
Leverage satisfied customers as brand ambassadors to drive organic growth through trusted recommendations.

### Model
Two-sided incentive: Both referrer and referee receive benefits.

---

## 2. Referral Tiers

### Tier 1: Standard Referral

**Requirements:**
- Referred store must sign up and remain active for 3 months

**Benefits for Referrer:**
- 5TK discount/month on subscription (applied for 12 months)

**Benefits for Referee:**
- 25TK discount in first 2 months
- Free onboarding support

**Example:**
```
Current Customer (A) → Refers Store B
Store B signs up → Pays 50TK/month
After 3 months activation → Customer A gets 5TK/month discount
Store B gets 25TK/month off for first 2 months
```

---

### Tier 2: Premium Referral

**Requirements:**
- Referred store must sign up AND remain active for 6 months
- Referred store must be in different geographic region

**Benefits for Referrer:**
- 10TK discount/month on subscription (applied for 24 months)

**Benefits for Referee:**
- 50TK discount in first 3 months
- Priority onboarding support
- Complimentary staff training session

**Example:**
```
Current Customer (C) → Refers Store D (different city)
Store D signs up → Pays 50TK/month
After 6 months activation → Customer C gets 10TK/month discount
Store D gets 50TK/month off for first 3 months
```

---

### Tier 3: Ambassador Referral

**Requirements:**
- Referrer must have 3+ successful referrals
- Referred store must sign up AND remain active for 12 months
- Referred store must provide positive feedback (NPS ≥ 8)

**Benefits for Referrer:**
- 15TK discount/month on subscription (applied for 36 months)
- Priority support access
- Early access to new features
- Exclusive Ambassador badge on account

**Benefits for Referee:**
- 75TK discount in first 6 months
- VIP onboarding with dedicated support
- Complimentary advanced training
- Feature request priority

## 3. Program Rules

### Eligibility

**Who Can Refer:**
- Any active paying customer for 30+ days
- Account in good standing (no outstanding issues)
- No duplicate or self-referrals

**What Qualifies:**
- Store owner signs up through referral link/code
- Store completes onboarding within 30 days
- Store remains active (makes sales) for required period

**What Doesn't Qualify:**
- Self-referrals (owning two stores, both signing up)
- Inactive referrals (signed up but never used)
- Referred stores that cancel before qualifying period
- Fraudulent activity

---

### Geographic Restrictions

**Same-Region Rule:**
- Referrals within the same city/region receive reduced benefits
- Reason: Prevents market cannibalization among local partners

**Example:**
```
Customer A in Dhaka → Refers Store B in same Dhaka neighborhood
→ Tier 1 benefits only (no geographic bonus)

Customer A in Dhaka → Refers Store B in Chittagong
→ Tier 2 benefits eligible (different region)
```

---

### Anti-Fraud Measures

**Prevention Methods:**
1. Unique referral code per customer
2. IP tracking for duplicate sign-ups
3. Phone number verification required
4. Manual review for suspicious patterns (e.g., mass sign-ups)

**Penalties for Violations:**
- First offense: Warning + forfeiture of tier benefits
- Second offense: Permanent disqualification from program
- Severe cases: Account suspension

## 4. Referral Process

### Step 1: Generate Referral Link

**Customer Action:**
1. Log into Lucky Store POS account
2. Navigate to "Refer a Friend" section
3. Click "Get Referral Link"
4. Copy unique link/code

**System Action:**
- Generates unique referral code: `LUCKY-{customerID}-{random}`
- Creates tracking link: `https://luckystorepos.com/ref/xxx`
- Sends link via email to customer

---

### Step 2: Referee Signs Up

**Referee Action:**
1. Click referral link
2. Complete registration form
3. Enter referral code (if not automatic)
4. Complete onboarding

**System Action:**
- Associates referral code with new account
- Sends confirmation email to referrer: "Store X signed up"
- Starts 30-day onboarding countdown

---

### Step 3: Activation Period

**Required Timeline:**
- Activation period: 30-90 days (based on referral tier)
- Store must be active and making sales

**Referrer Notifications:**
```
Day 1: "Store X signed up via your referral"
Day 7: "Store X completed onboarding"
Day 30: "Store X still active - 20 days remaining"
Day 60: "Referral qualification period ends in 20 days"
Day 90: "Congratulations! Referral qualified for benefits"
```

**Referee Notifications:**
```
Day 1: "Welcome! Your discount begins today"
Day 7: "Reminder: Complete staff training"
Day 30: "Your discount: 80 days remaining"
```

---

### Step 4: Benefit Application

**Automatic Application:**
- Discounts automatically applied to referrer's subscription
- Visible in account billing section

**Manual Application (if needed):**
- Referrer submits request via support
- Manual verification
- Backdated application if eligible

## 7. Customer Support

### Common Questions

**Q: When do I get my discount?**
A: "Your discount applies automatically once the referred store has been active for 3 months (6 months for Tier 2, 12 months for Tier 3)."

**Q: Can I refer my own second store?**
A: "No, self-referrals are not eligible. The referral program is designed to bring new, independent stores into the Lucky Store ecosystem."

**Q: What if the referred store cancels early?**
A: "If a referred store cancels before the qualification period, their referral doesn't qualify. No benefits are applied for that referral."

**Q: Is there a limit to how many referrals I can make?**
A: "No limit! The more stores you refer, the more discounts you earn. Top referrers can earn up to 15TK/month (180TK/year)."

**Q: What if my referred store doesn't like the system?**
A: "We'll work together to ensure they have a great experience. If they still decide to cancel, the referral won't qualify — but that's a learning opportunity for us both."

## 8. Program Changes & Terminations

### Right to Modify

We reserve the right to:
- Change benefit tiers and amounts (with 30-day notice)
- Update qualification periods
- Modify eligibility requirements
- Terminate the program entirely

### Grandfathering

**Existing Referrals:**
- Customers eligible for Tier 1+ benefits are grandfathered into current rates for 12 months
- After 12 months, rates adjust to new program (with 60-day notice)

**Referred Stores:**
- Original discount terms honored regardless of program changes
- Referral link remains valid for its lifetime
